CHARACTER

CREATIVITY – My head is full of ideas, so I often express them through design; I own a wood workshop where I go to design and create. I also enjoy painting and crafting.

HONESTY – I am a transparent individual. I tell the truth, and I am diplomatic in my approach. I try my best to keep my word because it is important to me.

RESPECT – Respect is reciprocal, so I address everyone with it. I do not raise my voice at people, and I carry myself in the way I want to be addressed.

COURAGE – I am courageous in nature, I take calculated risks, and I enjoy new experiences.

PASSION – I am driven by my passion to excel and love, I put my heart into all I do, and I chase my goals without relenting.

ENVIRONMENTALISM – This side of me grows with each passing day. I see the opportunity to contribute to every environment positively, and I preach when I can to help people see why we need to be conscious about the smallest things, like leaving water running. I am still learning, but it is good to be conscious. I also own three lovely thriving houseplants.
